Ítaca

iOS app for self service beer bars

The project consists of the design of a new app to let the customers pay through their mobiles. The customers earn points for each payment and this lets them upgrade their status.

Based on the company guidelines, with blue as the corporate colour, we started creating some high fidelity prototypes of an iOS native app, directly on Sketch, working closely with developers and also the client.

Navigation is so simple, we opted for a tab bar menu, with the three most important sections. In Balanç most of the action takes place, here you can check your expenses and your current balance. Once you add credit, it takes you to another screen where you can check your progress, the actual status (based on a little gamification game by levels) and how much points do you earn.

It's up to you and your liver to get ZEUS level!
